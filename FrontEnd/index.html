Demo Results Summary

The states of the different rooms throughout the gym are simulated using QNX, this page provides a summary of the simulation. The simulation is used too keep track of each rooms current state, throught a tracking period, the simulationj may encounter emergencies. These emergencies occur when a component state is out of a threshold where it is deemed safe. An example of this could be the sauna temperature rising too high, the temperature is tracked and an emergency is logged to the simulation.

After the simulation has completed its runtime, the information loggerd throughout the session is inputted into a textfile. The textfile is then parsed ont this site to provide a summary of the simulation.

The most pertinant information is the rate of emergencies by area. Each room is shown with the amount of emergencies that have occured throughout the runtime.
